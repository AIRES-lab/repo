Artificial Intelligence Research for Experimental Security

AIRES Lab studies how artificial intelligence can strengthen security research through generative agents, computational experimentation, and rigorous socio-technical analysis.

About AIRES Get in Touch

Lab focus

Empowering security studies with artificial intelligence

AIRES Lab develops and evaluates AI-enabled methods for studying security problems where technical systems, institutions, information environments, and human behavior interact. The lab is especially interested in generative agents, experimental simulation, computational social science, and responsible AI use in security research.
